About Us

At Mum's Story, we provide our target demographic with a friendly platform to share their life-experiences with each other about newborns.

We also provide services such as an "Online Doctor" and a store with shipping.

Sponsorship

Our organisation focuses on providing free services to all parents-alike. As such we do not have a strong source of profit. To help sustain our website, feel free to sponsor and be recognised by the community at our honorable mentions.
