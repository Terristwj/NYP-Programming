Little Tips

Scientists estimate that there are over a million spieces of fungi on Earth. However, only about 10% of them have been identified and named

Some fungi can help fight cancer! Scientists in Japan discovered that a particular community had unusually low cancer rates. Studies indicated that the members of this community ate a lot of Enokitake mushrooms. These mushrooms produce a substance in the body that helps to prevent cancer.
