Mountain Climbing

Taiwan is a mountainous country, with terrain ranging from beautiful forested hill to soaring peaks.

Prime trekking areas include Alishan, where there is an extensive network of hiking trails offer everything from day hikes to serious ascent trekking. Highly recommended is the 10 hour circult to Fengshan, or the easy dawn hike to the summit of Chuahan to watch the sunrise.

Shopping at Taipei 101

At 508 meters (1,671 feet), it is the tallest building in the world. It is a classy shopping center (Taipei 101 Mall) packed with expensive designer-label clothing stores and luxury restaurants. On the fourth floor is a magnificent bookstore (Page One) and the chance to rest tired feet in one of several coffeeshops. Ride the escalator down to the B1 level for Taipet's biggest and finest food court, and to one of the best-stocked supermarkets in Taiwan (Jason's Marketplace), full of international food products that are hard to find elsewhere on the island.
