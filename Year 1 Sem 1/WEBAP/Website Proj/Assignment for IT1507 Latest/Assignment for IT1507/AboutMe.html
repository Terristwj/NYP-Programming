About Me


Hi! My name is Terris, and I am a student of Nanyang Polytechnic from School of Information Technology.

My website is aimed to help motivate and spread awareness of being healthy. Especially in McDonalds.

In today's society, not many are convinced to keep themselves healthy. Slowy, but steadily, more teenagers and working adults are being inclined to higher risk of potential health issues such as high blood pressure, diabetes, heart diseases, and even cancer.
All these medical problems leads from the same factor: Obesity.
